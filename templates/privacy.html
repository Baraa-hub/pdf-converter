Privacy Policy

ieatpdf.com · Last updated: April 17, 2026
🔒 GDPR Compliant · No data stored · No tracking

This Privacy Policy explains how ieatpdf.com ("we", "our", or "us") handles your data when you use our PDF conversion service. We are committed to protecting your privacy and processing your data in full compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Who We Are

ieatpdf.com is a free online PDF conversion tool. We are the data controller for any personal data processed through this website.

For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact us at: admin@ieatpdf.com

2. What Data We Process

When you use ieatpdf.com, we temporarily process the following:

  • Uploaded PDF files — solely to perform the conversion you requested
  • Output files — the converted file (DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, HTML, JPG, PNG) generated from your PDF
  • Basic server logs — standard HTTP request logs (IP address, timestamp, request path) retained by our hosting provider (Railway) for up to 7 days for security and debugging purposes
✅ We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
✅ We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics scripts.
✅ We do not share your files or data with any third parties.

3. How We Use Your Files

Your uploaded files are used exclusively to perform the conversion you requested. The legal basis for this processing is Article 6(1)(b) GDPR — processing necessary to perform a service you have requested.

4. Data Retention — File Deletion

We do not retain your files. Here is the exact lifecycle of your data:

  • Your PDF is uploaded to a temporary location on our server
  • The conversion is performed immediately
  • The original PDF is deleted immediately after conversion, regardless of whether it succeeded or failed
  • The converted output file is deleted immediately after it is sent to your browser
  • Temporary processing files (e.g. page images) are deleted immediately after conversion
🗑️ No file is retained on our servers for more than a few seconds. Our server runs on an ephemeral filesystem — all data is permanently wiped on every server restart.

5. Cookies

ieatpdf.com does not use cookies of any kind — not for authentication, analytics, advertising, or preferences. No consent banner is required because no cookies are set.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website is hosted on Railway (railway.app). Railway may process standard server logs (IP addresses, request timestamps) as part of providing hosting infrastructure. Railway's privacy policy can be found at railway.app/legal/privacy.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics platforms (e.g. Google Analytics), or social media integrations.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

As a data subject under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — request a copy of any data we hold about you
  • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data (note: files are already deleted automatically)
  • Right to rectification — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data
  • Right to data portability — receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to lodge a complaint — complain to your local data protection authority

Since we do not retain any files or personal data, most of these rights are satisfied automatically. For any requests, please contact us at the address below.

8. Data Security

We take appropriate technical measures to protect your data during processing:

  • All connections to ieatpdf.com are encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Files are stored in isolated temporary directories with unique random IDs
  • No file can be accessed by other users
  • Files are deleted immediately after use

9. International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in the United States (us-west-2) via Railway hosting. By using this service, you acknowledge that your file will be temporarily processed on servers located outside the EU. This transfer is necessary to perform the service you requested and is covered under Article 49(1)(b) GDPR (transfer necessary for the performance of a contract).

10. Children's Privacy

ieatpdf.com is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
